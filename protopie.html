Define ProtoPie Cloud's Workflow

Before launching Cloud, ProtoPie supported a limited range of design workflows: ideation - prototype - deliver. From our company visits and casual interviews with design teams, we realize that supporting the iterative design process and concept evaluation are the core experiences as a team of designers using ProtoPie. Also, we found out that there is a strong need for managing these experiences for design leads or managers.

Before launching Cloud, users maximize their prototyping in a minimal portion of a design workflow: prototyping. To support the iterative design process and user testing for tech designers, we believed it is essential to develop the features for collaboration and user testing.

1. Version Management and Comment

Compared to the previous pie page, new Cloud offered For a Pie page in Team Space, team members can track a Pie's changes to check iterations with easier access. Besides, a built-in comment system enables them to leave feedbacks.

Prototypes shine when designers use those to validate their idea. Designers love the expressiveness of prototypes they made with ProtoPie and actively use those for their user testings. We created a suite of precise share display options for user's testing purposes.
